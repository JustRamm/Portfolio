About Me

Hi, I’m Abiram. On paper, I’m a final-year B.Tech Information Technology student. In practice, I’m a builder who refuses to choose between "making it work" and "making it feel good." I sit right at the intersection where code meets creativity. Whether I’m scripting immersive mechanics for a game, training intelligent AI agents, or architecting a full-stack app, my goal is always the same: The Experience.

I believe the best software doesn't just solve a problem—it tells a story. I bring a product mindset to engineering, meaning I care just as much about the user journey and pixel-perfect design as I do about clean architecture and efficient algorithms.

If you’re looking for a developer who speaks fluent "Engineer" but thinks like a "Creator," let’s talk.
